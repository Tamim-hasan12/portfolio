About Me

Quality is not an act,
it's a habit.

I'm Md. Tamim Hasan, a Junior SQA Engineer at AKIJ iBOS, currently testing cloud-based SaaS production systems — including HR Payroll, ERP, and POS platforms. I thrive in fast-paced Agile environments where structured test thinking protects real users in production.

What pulled me toward QA wasn't chasing bugs for the sake of it — it was realizing that behind every module I test, there are real people running their business on it: an HR manager processing salaries, a store owner tracking inventory. That responsibility is what keeps me deliberate about every test case I write and every edge case I chase down before it reaches production.

My testing scope covers the full quality lifecycle — from manual test case design and regression cycles to API testing with Postman and load testing with k6. I'm now expanding into automation with Playwright to bring speed and reliability to regression coverage.

I'm currently completing my B.Sc. in Computer Science and Engineering at Daffodil International University (expected 2026), with a focus on Software Engineering, Databases, and SQA principles.
